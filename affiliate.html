Affiliate FAQ

Who can become an affiliate?

Anyone with an audience that would benefit from our products! Bloggers, influencers, educators, content creators, and website owners are welcome to apply.

Is there a cost to join?

No! Our affiliate program is completely free to join. There are no signup fees or hidden costs.

What is the cookie duration?

We offer a generous 60-day cookie duration. If someone clicks your link and purchases within 60 days, you earn the commission.

When and how do I get paid?

Payments are processed monthly via PayPal or bank transfer. The minimum payout threshold is $50.

Can I promote on social media?

Absolutely! You can promote our products on any platform where you have an audience - blogs, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, TikTok, and more.

Do you provide promotional materials?

Yes! We provide banners, social media graphics, product images, email swipes, and sample content to make promoting easy and effective.
