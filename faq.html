Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do I download my purchased products?

+
After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with download links to all your products. You can also log into your account and access your purchases from the "My Downloads" section. All products are available for unlimited downloads, so you can re-download them anytime.

What file formats are the products available in?

+
Most of our products are available in PDF format, which is compatible with all devices. Some products may also include editable formats like DOCX, XLSX, or PSD files. The specific formats are listed on each product page.

Can I print the digital products?

+
Absolutely! All our products are designed to be printable. You can print them at home or take them to a professional printing service. We recommend using high-quality paper for the best results.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption for your safety.

What is your refund policy?

+
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. No questions asked!

I didn't receive my download email. What should I do?

+
First, check your spam/junk folder. If you still can't find it, log into your account and visit the "My Downloads" page. If you're still having issues, contact our support team at support@blearners.com with your order number.

Can I share the products with friends or colleagues?

+
Our products are licensed for personal use only. Sharing, distributing, or reselling our products is not permitted. If you'd like to purchase for a team or classroom, please contact us about our bulk licensing options.

Do I need an account to make a purchase?

+
No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to easily access all your purchases, track orders, and receive exclusive offers and early access to new products.

Are your products compatible with tablets and mobile devices?

+
Yes! All our products work on tablets, smartphones, and computers. PDF files can be opened with any PDF reader app, and editable files work with compatible apps like Microsoft Office or Google Docs.

How long do I have access to my downloads?

+
Forever! Once you purchase a product, you have lifetime access to download it. We recommend downloading and saving your files in a safe location, but you can always re-download from your account if needed.

Do you offer discounts for bulk purchases?

+
Yes! We offer special pricing for schools, businesses, and bulk orders. Please contact us at sales@blearners.com with details about your needs, and we'll create a custom package for you.

How do I update my account information?

+
Log into your account and go to "Account Settings." From there, you can update your email, password, billing information, and communication preferences.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Our support team is here to help!
