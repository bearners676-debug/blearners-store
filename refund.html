Our Commitment to You

At B-earners, we're committed to providing high-quality digital products that deliver real value. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase, which is why we offer a straightforward and generous refund policy.

Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of purchase
  • You purchased the product directly from our website
  • The product has a technical issue that we cannot resolve
  • You received a product different from what was advertised
  • You're not satisfied with the product for any reason

Refund exclusions:

  • Requests made after 30 days from the purchase date
  • Products purchased through third-party platforms or resellers
  • Bundle purchases where individual items have been used (partial refunds may be considered)
  • Cases of policy violation or fraudulent activity

How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple and straightforward:

Step 1: Contact Us

Send an email to refunds@blearners.com or use our contact form with the following information:

  • Your order number
  • Email address used for purchase
  • Product name(s)
  • Brief reason for the refund request (optional but helpful)

Step 2: Confirmation

We'll acknowledge your refund request within 24 hours (business days).

Step 3: Processing

Once approved, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The funds will be returned to your original payment method.

Refund Processing Time

After we approve your refund:

  • PayPal: 1-2 business days
  • Credit Card: 5-10 business days (depending on your card issuer)
  • Bank Transfer: 5-10 business days

Please note that processing times may vary depending on your financial institution.

Partial Refunds

In certain situations, partial refunds may be granted:

  • Bundle purchases where you only want to return specific items
  • If you've used a significant discount code (refund may be prorated)
  • Cases evaluated on an individual basis

Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical difficulties with a product:

  1. Contact Support First: We'll work quickly to resolve any technical issues
  2. Alternative Format: We may offer the product in a different format
  3. Full Refund: If we cannot resolve the issue, you'll receive a full refund

Digital Product Nature

Please understand that all our products are digital downloads. Once downloaded, they cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense. However, we still honor our refund policy because we value your trust and satisfaction.

Fraudulent Claims

While we trust our customers, we reserve the right to:

  • Deny refund requests that appear fraudulent
  • Suspend accounts with excessive refund requests
  • Report suspected fraudulent activity to appropriate authorities

Special Promotions

Products purchased during special promotions or sales are subject to the same refund policy. However:

  • Refunds will be issued for the amount paid
  • Promotional codes or discounts cannot be transferred
  • Limited-time offers may have specific terms (clearly stated at purchase)

Affiliate Purchases

If you purchased through an affiliate link, the same refund policy applies. However, affiliate commissions may be adjusted accordingly.

Questions Before Purchasing?

To minimize the need for refunds, we encourage you to:

  • Review product descriptions and previews thoroughly
  • Check our FAQ section for common questions
  • Contact us before purchasing if you're unsure about a product
  • Read customer reviews and testimonials

Exchanges

We don't offer direct product exchanges. However, if you'd like a different product:

  1. Request a refund for your original purchase
  2. Purchase the desired product separately
  3. Or contact us to discuss custom solutions

Policy Updates

We may update this refund policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

Need Help with a Refund?

Our customer support team is here to assist you with any questions or concerns about refunds.

Response Time: Within 24 hours (business days)
