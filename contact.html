Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my purchased products?
After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with download links. You can also access your products anytime from your account dashboard.
What file formats are the products available in?
Most of our products are available in PDF format, which can be used on any device. Some products may also include editable formats like DOCX or XLSX.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, contact us for a full refund.
Can I print the digital products?
Absolutely! All our products are designed to be printable. You can print them at home or take them to a professional printing service.
Do you offer bulk or educational discounts?
Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders and educational institutions. Please contact us at sales@blearners.com for more information.
How can I become an affiliate partner?
We'd love to work with you! Visit our Affiliate Program page or contact us directly to learn about partnership opportunities and commission rates.
